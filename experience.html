Experience

This is my experience.

I have had quite a few jobs since high school, I've worked everywhere from a title company to pest control. One thing I've learned from working all my jobs is that if you put in the effort you'll be a top level performer wherever you are.

  1. Southern Utah Title Company (Archive Specialist)
    • As an Archive Specialist I was tasked with scanning physical files to the company's online database. I was also tasked with tracking which files had been scanned and queuing them for destruction.

  2. Conduit Repair Systems inc. (Warehouse Worker)
    • As a Warehouse Worker at Conduit Repair Systems Inc. I kept track of warehouse stock and helped fill orders for customers. I also created many different types of conduit pipe to restock the warehouse.

  3. Greenix Pest Control (Customer Retention Agent)
    • At Greenix Pest Control I worked with the retention department. I dealt mainly with customer issues that had lead to a desire to cancel services with the company. I was charged with developing customer relationships quickly in order to solve their issues and negotiate future services.

  4. Blue Raven Solar (Proposals Designer)
    • As a Proposals Designer at blue raven I use information provided by salesman to create a proposal for the customer. This includes creating a 3d model of their home with the Aurora design tool, calculating energy usage and offset, and organizing customer information in an easily understandable way for presentation to the homeowner.
